  1. CODEPEN
    Summary of resource:
    Codepen is a site that holds among other things, challenges to improve your web development skills. It also hosts plenty of projects by users to explore their capabilities.
    Body of your review:
    Codepen is a great resource to improve your skills, and explore the projects of others. I have no significant negative comments about it.
    Rating:3

  2. CSS-Tricks
    Summary of resource:
    CSS Tricks has a wealth of code snippets, tutorials, and posts regarding development of sites using CSS.
    Body of your review:
    CSS Tricks has much in the way of information that can help build sites, however much of this is more suited to designers than developers.
    Rating:2

  3. Lorem Ipsum Text Generator
    Summary of resource:
    This website allows for the automatic generation of non-repeating lorem ipsum text for creating exactly as much text as is needed in mockups.
    This allows for better managing of site structure while in development.
    Body of your review:
    Has use, but is not especially unique in its role. Other options are available.
    Rating:1

  4. Iamismile's Web Dev Resource List
    Summary of resource:
    This Github repository hosts links to over 150 different sites useful for web development.
    Body of your review:
    While this resource may not host much in the way of useful information itself, it does succeed in making a fast and convenient organized means of accessing plenty of tools across the intenet.
    If including the usefulness of the tools and descriptions it contains I would have to say this is the most helpful resource.
    Rating:5

  5. Stack Overflow
    Summary of resource:
    Stack overflow is a forum for discussing concepts and issues in specific fields primarily regarding programming. If you have had an issue its not unlikely someone has posted about it on stack overflow.
    Body of your review:
    Stack Overflow is invaluable in sourcing solutions and fixes. It is easy to search and often has highly experinced users.
    Rating:4
